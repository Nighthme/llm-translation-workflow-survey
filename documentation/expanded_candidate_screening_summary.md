## Main Implication

The expanded search successfully fills the human workflow gap identified in the first pilot screening. The review should now explicitly distinguish:

1. Model-centered workflow studies: APE, LQA, terminology, document-level context.
2. Human-centered workflow studies: cognitive load, translator effort, trust, professional comparison, and human-in-the-loop correction.
3. Boundary/background ethics studies: trust, responsibility, and professional accountability.
