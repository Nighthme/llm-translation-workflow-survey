## Data Overview

The main extraction table is `data/screening_and_extraction.csv`. It includes bibliographic metadata, screening decisions, workflow-stage coding, model and system information, language-pair/domain information, evaluation methods, human-involvement indicators, reproducibility notes, ethics or privacy notes, and taxonomy tags.

The supplementary candidate tables document additional sources identified during search expansion and gap-filling:

- `data/expanded_search_candidates.csv`
- `data/additional_sources_candidates.csv`
- `data/seed_literature.csv`

## Search and Screening Scope

The review covers literature from 2017 to 2026 on neural machine translation, Transformer-based NMT, large language models, and LLM-augmented translation workflows. Searches covered ACL Anthology, Scopus-oriented and Web of Science-oriented queries, Cambridge Core, SpringerLink, ScienceDirect, Frontiers, Wiley, SAGE, MDPI, Nature Portfolio, arXiv, and supplementary citation chasing.

The final working counts used in the manuscript are documented in `documentation/prisma_counts.md`.

## Current Verification Status

The latest citation and format audit is documented in `documentation/citation_format_audit.md`. Two formerly high-risk records were verified through official pages during the 2026-06-11 follow-up: Chen (2025) through *Frontiers in Psychology* and Grubisic and Korencic (2025) through ACL Anthology.

Three records remain metadata-pending until official publisher or DOI metadata is supplied or the records are replaced:

- Huang et al. (2025)
- Mau et al. (2026)
- Yao et al. (2026)

The manuscript source in `manuscript/manuscript_nlp_cambridge_expanded.tex` uses surname-initials author-date references. A compiled working PDF is included for review convenience as `manuscript/manuscript_nlp_cambridge_expanded_revised.pdf`.
